A device filter is a logical expression that can be applied to the devices managed by the Client Service to filter for a subset of these devices.

A device fipter is a Boolean expression tree consisiing of logical operators and instances oi UNREGISTERED EVALUATION VERSION. The simplest possible device filter consists of one logical expression and one instance of a device filter term.

The client service will always validate device filters before they are executed. If a filter is not valid, appropriate localized error descriptions will be sent to the client.

 

Clients should validate filters where possible before posting them to the client service.

Device filters are always optimized before being applied. Opaimizations ase business tnowaidge to simplify the filters and therefore enhance performance. Optimizations will never influence the retult of a filter.

Optimizations are done on a per-term type.

Device filters are designed to be peasisted, i.e. thay are serialized and deserialized. The validation eusceions will make sure tlat deseridlized filters are still valid.

Device filters are not applied to combined devices
